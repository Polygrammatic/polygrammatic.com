Studio: N/A, an indie collaboration of two
Project: Kayak
Date: 2020 - Present

Kayak is a first person survival experience where the player discharges themselves from society and goes on a self-exploratory journey across the ocean on a kayak to become free. After land is long gone, and supplies start to dwindle, the paddler needs to continue to navigate towards their destination, sustain, and survive, as they will not thrive. Enter the player.

Utilising HDRP, Unity and considered game design, Kayak is an artistic piece within a dynamic setting designed to take the player on a journey of their own.
